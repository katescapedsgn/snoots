why us

The team of snoots has been specializing in animal care since 1970. The trained staff goes through several courses even before attempting to care for pets. They are also licensed to do common medical procedures, as well as handle pets as trainers.

The facilities we have are also first-rate. The instruments are sterilized before use on each pet, and the equipment are the latest in technology. We have:

  • Grooming Bath 300
  • Nail clipper by Ceasar Melon
  • MasterShearers
  • Shadow Enclosers
  • Customer area where you can watch dogs being groomed.
